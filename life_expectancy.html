Life expectancy

Life expectancy constitutes a crucial indicator of the overall health and well-being of populations around the world. It is defined as the average number of years a person can expect to live, based on current mortality rates. As an essential metric, it assists policymakers, researchers, and health professionals in evaluating the effectiveness of healthcare systems, economic stability, and social factors such as nutrition and sanitation.
The depiction of life expectancy has undergone significant development over time, and current capabilities allow for the use of advanced data visualizations to explore these trends in great detail. The map presented here displays life expectancy across a range of countries, offering a snapshot of European health. It facilitates navigation through different countries, allowing the user to observe the life expectancy in those countries.
